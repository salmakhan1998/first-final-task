About Me

The job of a travel agent is quite exciting and fulfilling as you would be helping people to live some of the happiest moments of their life. Travel agents facilitate the travel arrangements for individuals and groups who are traveling for business or leisure. As a travel agent, you get to plan trips and itineraries for people who are seeking fun-filled holidays. You would be making travel arrangements for business clients, computing the expenses and costs, making bookings for hotels and accommodations, rental cars, excursions and other special events. You will be providing fare information and suggesting suitable destinations after analyzing the needs and specifications of the clients.
